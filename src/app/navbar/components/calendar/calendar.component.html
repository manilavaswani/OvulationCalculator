Fertility Tips:

  • The fertile window is when you're most likely to conceive.
  • Ovulation typically occurs 14 days before your next period.
  • The chances of getting pregnant are highest 2-3 days before ovulation and on the day of ovulation.
  • During the luteal phase, the chances of conception are very low if the egg wasn't fertilized during the fertile window.

Tips for Accurate Tracking

  • Track your basal body temperature for more precise ovulation prediction
  • Note changes in cervical mucus throughout your cycle
  • Use ovulation predictor kits for additional confirmation
